About the Site

Welcome

This website is a project done by the codewithtec software development company in Liberia. This project was carefully selected in 2019, to help strengthen the Liberian Culture and promote the local Languages in the country. We believe that all Liberian and others have the right to learn their local languages by creating a online site and applications is just one step in helping the process. THis site will help expand our learning environment for local languages.

User on this website have access to the following learning topics

  • Kpelle Short History
  • Alphabets & Numbers
  • Family Relationship
  • Vowel & Special Characters
  • Name of Fruit and Animals
  • Kpelle Translator
  • Dialog and Many others/li>

Enjoy Your stay and learn the languages of your mother land.
