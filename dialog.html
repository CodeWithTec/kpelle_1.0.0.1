Greeting in kpelle is one of the many way we are able to practice our skill in understanding the language. Note: In kpelle, there are lots of differences when having conversation as compared to english.

Greeting in Kpelle

Kpelle: Singular Ya ŋuŋ
English: Good Morning

Kpelle: Singular Àa naoi, ya ŋuŋ
English: Yes My friend, Good Morning

Kpelle: Plural Ka ŋuŋ
English: Good Morning to all

Kpelle: Plural Àa naoi, ka ŋuŋ
English: Yes, Good Morning my friend

Kpelle: Singular Ya tua
English: Good Afternoon/Good Evening

Kpelle: Singular Àa naoi, ya tua
English: Afternoon/Good Evening, my friend

Kpelle: Plural Ka tua
English: Good Afternoon/Good Evening to all

Kpelle: Plural Àa naoi, Ka tua
English: My Friend, Good Afternoon/Good Evening
